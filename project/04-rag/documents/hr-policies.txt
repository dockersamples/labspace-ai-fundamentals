Employee Benefits and Leave Policy

Vacation and PTO
Employees receive 20 days of paid time off (PTO) per year, accrued at a rate of 1.67 days per month. PTO can be used for vacations, personal days, or any other time away from work. Unused PTO of up to 10 days can be rolled over to the following year. Any unused PTO beyond 10 days is forfeited at year end.

Sick Leave
All employees receive 10 days of paid sick leave per year, separate from the PTO allowance. Sick leave does not roll over and cannot be converted to cash. For absences longer than three consecutive days, a doctor's note may be required.

Parental Leave
New parents are eligible for 16 weeks of fully paid parental leave, available to both birthing and non-birthing parents, including adoptive parents. Leave must be taken within 12 months of the child's birth or adoption finalization.

Remote Work Policy
All full-time employees may work remotely up to three days per week. Remote work days must be pre-approved by your direct manager. Employees must be available during core hours of 10am to 3pm in their local timezone. Full remote arrangements may be considered on a case-by-case basis for exceptional circumstances with VP-level approval.

Health and Wellness Benefits
The company provides comprehensive medical, dental, and vision insurance for employees and their eligible dependents. In addition, each employee receives a wellness stipend of $100 per month, which can be used for gym memberships, fitness classes, mental health apps, therapy, or other wellness-related expenses.

Performance Reviews and Compensation
Performance reviews are conducted twice per year, in June and December. Compensation adjustments and promotions are considered during the December review cycle. Employees may receive a merit increase of up to 10% based on performance ratings. Exceptional performers may also be eligible for a spot bonus at any time during the year.
